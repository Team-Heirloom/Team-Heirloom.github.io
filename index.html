The Problem

When a loved one passes, families are often unprepared—and far apart.

In one of life's most difficult moments, families are forced to figure everything out quickly.

But today's reality makes it even harder:

  • Family members often live in different cities—or across the country
  • Important details aren't centralized
  • Responsibilities aren't clearly defined

What should be a time to focus on each other becomes a scramble to manage logistics, decisions, and uncertainty.

Why This Matters Now

A massive generational shift is already underway.

Over the next decade, millions of families will face the realities of aging, loss, and transition.

The Baby Boomer generation is entering their late 70s and 80s

Families are more geographically dispersed than ever

Critical information is often incomplete, outdated, or inaccessible

When something happens, the burden doesn't fall on just one person—it impacts the entire family.

The Solution

Heirloom gives families clarity when they need it most.

Heirloom brings together your family's most important information, people, and responsibilities into one secure system.

From essential documents to key financial and personal details, everything is organized and accessible when it matters most, including:

  • Wills, Trusts and Life Insurance policies
  • Financial accounts and assets
  • Key contacts like attorneys, advisors, and accountants
  • Important documents, instructions and passwords

So when a loved one passes, your family has:

  • A clear understanding of what to do
  • Access to what matters
  • Confidence in how to move forward

No more searching through emails, files, or drawers to piece everything together. All stored securely and accessible only to the people you designate.
